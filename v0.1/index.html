Why our InnovSprints? 

Our InnovSprints are high energy, immersive problem-solving experiences designed to get the very best out of your people. Your employees will collaborate to create solutions that address real-world problems and opportunities in your business. Simultaneously they will foster empathy, critical thinking, curiosity, collaboration and communication skills.

Sprint Preparation is vital to the success of our InnovSprints. We advise on the themes/domains and the How Might We (HMW) framing that work best based on our collective experience. We help you make decisions on Sprint duration (may vary from a half-day to say 20 or 30 hours in total over several weeks), Sprint format (in-person, remote, blended), team formation, Sprint venue and the composition of your judging panel.
